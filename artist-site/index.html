About the Artist

I'm a digital artist blending collage traditions with machine learning tools. My practice explores how datasets remember, forget, and dream—reassembling fragments into synthetic memory.

Exhibitions include group shows in Berlin, NYC, and online festivals. Available for commissions, screenings, and collaborations.

  • Based in: Remote / Global
  • Mediums: Digital collage, AI video, sound
  • Tools: Photoshop, After Effects, Python, Stable Diffusion
